Information and Details about my Word Origins App

Title: Where'd that Word Come From?

Description: This app allows you to search for any word in English that you would like to learn about the origins of. It will return and display definitions and etymologies from Merriam-Webster's Dictionary. It will also show results from Wikipedia (if there is a wikipedia article with that entry), either a disambiguation or a page summary.

Finally, it will display 10 newspapers that contain the searched word that have been archived in the Library of Congress and are available online. The app gives you links to follow to view further information whenever relevant.

Technologies: HTML5, CSS3, Javascript, and jQuery.
